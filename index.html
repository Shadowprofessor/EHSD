EHSD

An energy harvesting system is needed to power electronic devices to autonomous operation in remote locations as well as to power up power efficient electronic gadgets and sensors by ambient energy from sources like sunlight, living plants/trees, or heat etc. This energy harvesting system eliminates traditional power sources like chemical batteries which is hazardous to the environment.
The aim of this research lab is to develop new technologies that can capture electrical energy from living plants and tress apart from sunlight or movement. The photosynthesis activity in trees and plant converted into usable electricity to power small electronic devices autonomously to enable the development of self-powered sensors, wearables, and IoT devices with extended lifespans while reducing reliance on traditional power sources and minimizing environmental impact.
EHSD research lab aims to develop integrated solutions for renewable energy storage systems with higher efficiency. The research lab also focuses on the development of hybrid renewable energy system to harvest energy using photosynthesis and photovoltaic techniques. We are also working to develop an eco-friendly, rechargeable and regenerative Herbal batteries as next-gen power source for electronic gadgets, sensors, and other future devices.

Vision

To lead towards a 100% carbon-free future energy system.

Mission

To lead the advancement of sustainable energy solutions through innovative research, development, and application of energy harvesting technologies. We aim to harness renewable energy from diverse sources, including solar, wind, thermal, and bioenergy, to create efficient, eco-friendly systems that power the future.

Our mission is to foster interdisciplinary collaboration,and reduce the corbon emission by transforming how energy is generated, stored and utilized.
